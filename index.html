The PJC Card

The PJC Card offers guaranteed access to 25 or 50 hours of time on our fleet of super-mid-size business jets or very light jets, anywhere in the US, with 24 hours notice. Buy the card and add, renew, or cancel at any time. All customer deposits are held in a transparent segregated account and there’s no long-term commitment.
